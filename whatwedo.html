Quote from a member

Unfortunately, trash is still a major problem in the parks of Copenhagen

At first glance the parks in Copenhagen look clean and nice. However, this is only due to the combined efforts of volunteers and public services. Public services are limited, and they are currently not able to handle it all. This is where we come in. We know the areas that are left out, and we target them to have the best impact.

Fun activity for families

Believe it or not, picking up litter is actually fun. It's an activity we wholeheartedly recommend for yourself, your family and your peers. It can have instant results for the neighborhood and feels rewarding. Best of all, you get to meet likeminded and fun people that are not only there to help, but to have a great time.

Who can join?

Anyone who enjoys clean and beautiful public spaces are welcome to join us.

What do I need to bring?

  • Large plastic bags
  • Gloves for personal safety and Covid measures
  • Optional pick-up stick
