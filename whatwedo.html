We are needed in the parks

Litter in the parks of Copenhagen

At first glance the parks in Copenhagen look clean and nice. However, this is only due to the combined efforts of volunteers and public services.

Public services are limited, and they are not able to handle it all. This is where we come in. We know the parks, and we go for the best impact.

Fun activity for families

Believe it or not, picking up litter is actually fun. It's an activity we wholeheartedly recommend for yourself and your peers.

Who can join?

Anyone who enjoys clean and beautiful public spaces are welcome to join us.

What to bring?

• Large plastic bags
• Gloves for personal safety and Covid measures
• Optional pick-up stick
