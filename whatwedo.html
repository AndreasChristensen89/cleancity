Quote from a member

Trash in the parks of Copenhagen

At first glance the parks in Copenhagen look clean and nice.
However, this is only due to the combined efforts of volunteers and public services. Public services are limited, and they are currently not able to handle it all.
This is where we come in. We know the areas that are left out, and we target them to have the best impact.

Fun activity for families

Believe it or not, picking up litter is actually fun. It's an activity we wholeheartedly recommend for yourself, your family, and your peers.

Who can join?

Anyone who enjoys clean and beautiful public spaces are welcome to join us.

What do I need to bring?

Large plastic bags

Gloves for personal safety and Covid measures

Optional pick-up stick
