Who We Are

We work hard to create a cleaner city for every generation

We are a group of volunteers and our mission is simple: to create a better environment for living!

We provide the everyday citizen, adults and children alike, the opportunity to impact the city they live in.

Doing Good Together

Our resources are limited but we make up for it with passionate people, good group-dynamics, and a collective desire for change. We operate in the parks of Copenhagen.

Please don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions, or if you wish to join us.
